In twenty seventeen, one of the most significant events was the acquisition of 51% majority stake in Lotus by Geely, a Chinese automotive company. This investment provided Lotus with a new level of financial stability and resources for future growth and development. At this year in terms of product announcements, Lotus introduced the Lotus Evora GT430 and GT430 Sport.

In twenty eighteen It is l lotus seventieth anniversary, while there might not have been any groundbreaking model launches, it was a year that set the stage for future developments. Geely's investment started to show its influence in two thousand eighteen. This involvement brought new resources and opportunities for Lotus to expand its product lineup and explore new technologies, including electric and hybrid.

In twenty nineteen was a year of innovation and expansion for Lotus, with the developing of the groundbreaking type 130 Evija hypercar, investments in manufacturing, and a blend of performance-focused and forward-looking initiatives.

In twenty twenty, COVID-19 Pandemic Impact, like many industries, Lotus was affected by it. Production and business operations were temporarily disrupted due to lockdowns and supply chain challenges. Even that Lotus announced plans to invest in upgrading and expanding its Hethel headquarters and manufacturing facilities. <u>These investments were aimed at enhancing production capabilities and accommodating the production of new and upcoming models, including electric and hybrid vehicles.</u>

In twenty twenty one, Lotus launched the Type 131 sports car, which is also known as the Lotus Emira. The Emira was positioned as a significant step forward for Lotus, combining modern design, performance, and technology.

In two thousand twenty two, an E-segment SUV codenamed Type 132 debuted The all-new and all-electric Lotus Eletre is a striking and progressive Hyper SUV.
