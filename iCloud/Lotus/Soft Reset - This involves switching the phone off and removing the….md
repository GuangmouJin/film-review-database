Soft Reset - This involves switching the phone off and removing the SIM, then switching the phone on without the SIM inside, switching back-off and replacing the SIM before switching-on again. This will have the effect of forcing the SIM to connect to the network and then update.



Manual Roam - This involves using your network settings to manually roam off the Vodafone network and back onto it. Each phone will differ slightly, but typically the steps are found in Settings and then Network Settings. We are looking to manually roam for networks and in selecting this the phone will search for all available networks. Once they present please try to select a network other than Vodafone. The phone will not be able to connect, but it will have taken the phone off the Vodafone network. Please then manually roam back onto the Vodafone network.
