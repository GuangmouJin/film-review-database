Hi, I’m Mike. I’m originally from China, and I’m based in London now. I came to the UK in 2020 for my master’s degree at Coventry University London Campus and I graduated in 2021 with a Distinction. 

Before I came to the UK, I had seven years IT support experience,  working with HP in Malaysia and a real estate company in China. During that time, I mainly focused on first-line support, onsite support and helpdesk team leadership.
 
After I graduated from my master, I joined a company where I provided IT support for Alibaba group, and I worked there for about a year, supporting over 500 users across 10 offices. My responsibilities included daily IT support, onboarding and offboarding processes, and video conferencing meeting and event support.
 
In my last job, I worked in Lotus Car as an IT Operations Specialist. At that time, the London office had just been set up, and I was the only IT member onsite. My manager and the rest of the IT team were based in Coventry. We have a design company in Coventry. My main responsibilities can be divided into six parts.

First is daily onsite support, that included supporting 800 users, both software and hardware. I also need to support office equipment, like hotdesk, printer, and network devices. I also need to support meeting and event. We have some internal product launch events and town halls. 

Secondly, I was the global IT asset management owner, overseeing 2,500 assets. taking full responsibility for lifecycle management, covering inventory control, procurement, asset registration, asset tracking, auditing, and disposal.

Thirdly, I was in charge of the onboarding and off-boarding processes, it is not only preparing new starter accounts access and devices, arranging the IT induction, and managing asset for the leaver, but also I need to communicated with the HR team design the all process.

Finally, I acted as a BPM system coordinator. I need to respond the business requirements, work with Internal Control team to reduce the risk, and collaborated with engineers to design and improve business processes.

Unfortunately, the business not running well. at the end of last year, the company decided to close the London office and relocate to Coventry. Although I was offered a relocation opportunity, I wasn’t planning to leave London at that time, so I decided to move on and look for new opportunities here.




Finaly, I was also the contact person for our Helpdesk vendor. We have a Helpdesk team to support our users. We had weekly review meetings to improve their SLA performance.
